About us

FoodVilla is an online recipe as well as ingredient ordering app that is not only a reference portal but also it includes several other features based on inputs that we have gathered from users and after hearing what they are really looking for in an ideal app. Thus, our main aim is to help a customer not only find a recipe easily but at the same time also lets him/ her order the ingredients online. As soon as a user browses through any recipe, we will immediately throw a pop up listing out all the ingredients and allow a user to place an order . A user is given the flexibility of adding deleting from the shopping list that is auto populated. This integrated platform helps a user to easily order the ingredients without even having to go to a different online site or even physically to a supermarket to get the ingredients.
